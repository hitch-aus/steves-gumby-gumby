What is Gumby Gumby

Gumby Gumby (Pittosporum angustifolium) is a native Australian plant that grows across various regions of the country, including parts of Queensland.

It is known as a traditional bushfood and has long been part of Aboriginal knowledge systems. Like many native plants, it is closely connected to place, environment and cultural context.

Today, Gumby Gumby is commonly prepared from the dried leaf, including in capsule form. Preparation methods can vary, but often focus on keeping the plant as close to its natural form as possible.

How we prepare it

At Steve's Gumby Gumby, our Gumby Gumby is carefully identified and harvested locally near Clermont, Queensland and prepared in small batches.

The leaves are naturally dried and then cool-milled with minimal intervention.

We aim to keep the process simple and consistent, while making it available at a reasonable price.
