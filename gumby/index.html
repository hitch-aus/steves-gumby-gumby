What is Gumby Gumby

Gumby Gumby (Pittosporum angustifolium) is a native Australian plant that grows across various regions of the country, including parts of Queensland.

As a traditional bushfood, Gumby Gumby is part of long-held Aboriginal knowledge, connected to country and environment.

Today, Gumby Gumby is most often prepared from the dried leaf, including in capsule form. We keep the process simple, with minimal handling, so the plant stays as close to its natural state as possible.

How we prepare it

Our Gumby Gumby is carefully identified and harvested locally near Clermont, Queensland and prepared in small batches.

The leaves are naturally dried and then cool-milled with minimal intervention.

We aim to keep the process simple and consistent, while making it available at a reasonable price.
