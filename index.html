Our Approach

Our Gumby Gumby is carefully identified and harvested locally in Clermont and processed with minimal intervention to help preserve its natural phytochemicals. The leaves are naturally dried and then cool-milled.

These capsules are offered based on personal experience and feedback from people who have used Gumby Gumby. We aim to keep them available at a reasonable price so they remain accessible for all who need them.

We encourage everyone to do their own research when considering any supplements.
