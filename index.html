Our Approach

Our Gumby Gumby is carefully identified and harvested locally near Clermont, Queensland and processed with minimal intervention. The leaves are naturally dried and then cool-milled.

In late 2025 we were entrusted with continuing this small local operation after Steve stepped away. Steve had been preparing Gumby Gumby in Clermont for over 13 years and has generously shared his knowledge with us.

We encourage everyone to do their own research when considering any supplements.
